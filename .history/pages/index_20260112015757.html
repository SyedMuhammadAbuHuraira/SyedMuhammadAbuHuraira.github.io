Research Leadership

Jordan Terry founded and leads the Farama Foundation, whose reinforcement learning infrastructure powers AI research at Amazon, Google/DeepMind, Meta, Microsoft, and every major AI research organization globally — with 250+ million installations to date.

OpenAI transferred stewardship of OpenAI Gym — their first flagship project — to the Foundation. The Foundation also maintains the Arcade Learning Environment, the benchmark that led Google to acquire DeepMind.

Entrepreneurship & Industry

She previously founded Swarm Labs, a venture-backed AI security company that developed a novel AI-assisted vulnerability search tool for deployed AI systems.

The company was backed by technical leadership at OpenAI, Microsoft, Google, and HuggingFace. Jordan Terry also led teams using deep learning for embedded systems in intelligence applications at two major multinational defense contractors.
